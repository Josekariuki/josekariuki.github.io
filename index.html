Hx — Background

Two careers, one skill: reading signals carefully

I hold a degree in Clinical Medicine & Community Health from JKUAT and currently practise as a medical intern in Murang'a County, working across medical, surgical, paediatric, and obstetric wards. My days involve reviewing patient histories, auditing mortality cases, and presenting findings that change how care is delivered.

In parallel, I build with Python. Diagnosis and data science are the same discipline wearing different coats: gather evidence, weigh it honestly, commit to a decision, and audit the outcome. That's how I approach every model, dashboard, and pipeline I ship.

I'm most useful at the intersection — health analytics, clinical risk modelling, case management, and AI products that need someone who has actually worked the front line they're built for.
